Welcome to the Level Editor's Warehouse

This site has custom and official assets that have been collected over the years, and almost every single region to exist, apart from those that got lost to time.

The repo is being updated regularly, and new resources are being added every so often, and if you want to contribute to it by donating your tiles or region projects, you're welcome to shoot me a DM on Discord @solaristheworstcatever!
Or you can use the repo donations server under the discord icons.

And never forget to have fun or try to LOL! -Solar
